Frequently Asked Questions

When will URL scanning be available?

We're targeting Q1 2025 for the initial release. Sign up above to get notified when it launches.

Will URL scanning be free?

Yes! Just like our HTML analyzer, URL scanning will be completely free with no registration required.

What websites can I scan?

You'll be able to scan any publicly accessible website. Private or password-protected sites will require special configuration.

How is this different from the HTML analyzer?

URL scanning will analyze live websites directly, including dynamic content, while the HTML analyzer works with static code.
