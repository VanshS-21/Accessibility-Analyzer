🎓 Understanding Web Accessibility

💡 What is Web Accessibility?

Web accessibility ensures that websites and digital content are usable by people with disabilities. This includes visual, auditory, motor, and cognitive impairments.

🌐 Why It Matters

Over 15% of the world's population has some form of disability. Accessible websites benefit everyone and are often required by law in many countries.

📋 WCAG Guidelines

Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG 2.1) provide international standards. Level AA is the legal standard in most jurisdictions.

🔧 Common Issues

Missing alt text, poor color contrast, unlabeled forms, and improper heading structure are among the most common accessibility problems.
