Frequently Asked Questions

What is WCAG 2.1 AA?

WCAG 2.1 AA (Web Content Accessibility Guidelines) is the globally accepted standard for web accessibility, established by the W3C. It ensures websites are perceivable, operable, understandable, and robust for all users, including those with visual, auditory, motor, or cognitive disabilities. Level AA compliance is required by most accessibility laws worldwide, including the ADA, Section 508, and EN 301 549.
